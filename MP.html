Join Us in Molecular Profiling (MP)

We are using "microsampling" technology to better understand COVID-19. Join us if you are interested!

How It Works

01

You must be live near Stanford to participate in the microsampling study.

02

Complete the consent and enrollment process online.

03

You will receive a microsampling kit. The kit contains supplies to collect fingerprick microsamples. See microsampling instructions and video.

04

You will collect microsamples at home and complete an online symptom survey.

05

Drop off the microsamples at Stanford.

06

If you have a wearable health tracker, you are encouraged to concurrently enroll in the wearables study.
