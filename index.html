Track and Stay Informed by Personal Health Dashboard

My Personal Health Dashboard (MyPHD) is a tool to explore your health data while helping to advance science.

Who Are Eligible

We are trying to find out if information from wearable devices, like FitBit and Apple Watch, can be used to track infectious diseases like COVID-19.

Wearable Devices

To use MyPHD, you need at least one of the following compatible devices: FitBit, Apple Watch, Oura Ring, Motiv Ring, Garmin Watch, BioStrap, Dexcom CGM.

For iPhone Users

The MyPHD app is currently only for iPhone.

Healthcare Workers

Healthcare workers and high-risk individuals are especially encouraged to enroll in the study.

ALL DATA IN ONE PLACE

Integration with the latest technologies enables the real-time updates on your health-related data and even predict the onset of symptoms berehand.

AGGREAGATE AND VISUALIZE

By Aggregating the data from your different wearables within a certain time period, Personal Health Dashboard will present you with a synthezied version of your health data in multiple formats.

MULTIPLE DEVICES AVALABLE

We fit your need as you may possess multiple wearable devies. Enable any of them and select the main one that you would like to use.

MANAGE YOUR SYMPTOMS VIA CALENDAR

A calendar is included in the Personal Health Dashboard to streamline and reduce the friction of managing and tracing back your symptoms.
